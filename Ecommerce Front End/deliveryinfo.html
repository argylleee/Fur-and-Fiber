Delivery Information


Shipping Policy

We offer shipping within the Philippines. Please review our shipping policy below:

Shipping Methods

We provide the following shipping methods:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 2-3 business days
  • Overnight Shipping: Next-day delivery

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on the total weight of the products in your order and your location. The shipping cost will be displayed during the checkout process before you complete your purchase.

Order Processing Time

Please allow 1-2 business days for order processing before the shipment is dispatched.

Order Tracking

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number to track the status of your shipment on our website.

International Shipping

Unfortunately, we do not offer international shipping. Please check our newsletter for more updates.

Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have specific shipping restrictions due to legal or logistical limitations. Such restrictions will be mentioned on the product page if applicable.
